PDAC 2016 Geotech speakers list - The session will include speakers from Geotech Ltd. and industry experts who will present on a variety of case studies, trends and topics related to airborne EM geophysics.
Conditions for Outlook

We completed a 100,000 line-km survey in Fiji this winter. Geotech committed to training 20 local geologist and geophysicists to process, visualize and analyze VTEM data. Our cooperation with the federal government has augmented and enriched the geophysical information that future generations will rely on to find new sources of mineralizations and potential groundwater resources for potable drinking water.

Talks will also cover SPM effects and how Geotech has also applied a proprietary superparamagnetic (SPM) extraction algorithm for identifying and differentiating SPM-contaminated EM decays from true Ni-Cu-PGE target responses for its recent VTEM surveys. The discovery of this effect was first verified in our Greenland survey.
